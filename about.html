About Rosemary Solutions

We build intelligent products that feel natural.

Our Mission

At Rosemary Solutions, our mission is simple: create tools that turn complexity into clarity. We believe technology should feel intuitive — something that enhances your life, not something you have to fight with. Our products are built for people who want powerful capabilities without the friction.

Our Philosophy

We follow three guiding principles:

  • Simplicity first. The best tools disappear into the background and let you focus on what matters.
  • Privacy by design. Your data is yours. Our products minimize data collection, avoid ads, and never sell or trade your information.
  • Human-centered AI. Artificial intelligence should feel natural — like speaking to a helpful assistant, not interacting with a machine.

The Story Behind Rosemary

Rosemary Solutions was created with a single goal: build products that combine intelligence, design, and usability. After years of using productivity and finance tools that felt overwhelming or rigid, we set out to create something different — tools that feel personal and effortless.

That vision is reflected in our design language: clean, warm, and approachable. And it's brought to life through RosaFinance, our first product.

Our First Product: RosaFinance

RosaFinance is an AI-powered personal finance assistant designed to make managing money feel natural. You can talk to Rosa, type naturally, or snap a photo of any receipt. She understands multiple languages, organizes your transactions, and gives you clarity without linking bank accounts.

RosaFinance represents our philosophy: powerful capability, wrapped in simplicity.

What's Next

RosaFinance is only the beginning. We're committed to expanding our product family — building tools across finance, productivity, automation, and more. Each product will follow the same values: intelligent, intuitive, privacy‑focused, and beautifully designed.
