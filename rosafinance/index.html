Dictate, type, or share

Track spending in seconds‡ — just say "I spent €45 on groceries at Lidl" and Rosa handles the rest. She logs it, categorizes it, and updates your budget automatically.

Snap a receipt or share a screenshot from your bank app‡ — Rosa reads it and extracts all transactions. Share directly from Photos, Messages, or Safari without opening the app first.

Accepts input in multiple languages — type in Spanish, dictate in Dutch, snap a French receipt.

Learns Your Habits

The more you use Rosa, the better she understands you. No manual training needed — she improves automatically.

  • Smart flow placement — learns your patterns and places new transactions automatically
  • Duplicate detection — snap a receipt today, import your bank statement tomorrow — no double-counting
  • Refund handling — spots refunds and adjusts your records correctly
  • Context-aware learning — learns from your habits and transaction patterns (e.g., if a merchant you usually pay suddenly pays you, Rosa recognizes it as a refund)

Your rules, your way

Rosa works great out of the box, but you can customize how she handles your transactions. Write rules in plain language and Rosa optimizes and applies them automatically.‡

Tell her things like "Ignore transfers from Venmo", "Tag Netflix as Subscription", or "Always assign coffee shops to Leisure" — she'll follow your preferences every time.

Found in Settings → My Rules for Rosa

Simple Money Flows

Forget complex budgets with dozens of categories. Rosa uses just four flows — simple by design.

  • Income — what comes in
  • Spending — your needs (rent, groceries, bills)
  • Leisure — your wants (dining out, entertainment)
  • Saving — what you keep

Define your expected income, decide how much goes to each flow, and Rosa tracks your progress automatically. Every month resets — start fresh with clarity.
