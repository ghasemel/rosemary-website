Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 28, 2025

RosaFinance is a product by Rosemary Solutions. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, where we process it, how long we keep it, and your choices. If anything here conflicts with an app‑store specific disclosure, the stricter disclosure applies.

What We Collect

  • Your Email: We get this from Sign in with Apple or Google to set up your account.
  • Account IDs: Technical identifiers to keep your account secure and manage your subscription.
  • Purchase History: Your subscription details and payment history.
  • Financial Data: The expenses, income, budgets, and financial records you create in the app.
  • Chat Messages: All text messages you send to Rosa, including questions and instructions. We keep your chat history to provide context for future conversations.
  • Your Notes: Text notes you add and any feature requests or votes you submit.
  • Crash Reports: Technical diagnostics to fix bugs and improve the app.

What We Process (But Don't Keep)

  • Receipt Photos: Images you capture to automatically create transactions (receipts, bills, bank statements, or payment confirmations). We process these through our secure servers and immediately delete the originals.

How We Use Your Data

  • To Make the App Work: Sign you in, manage your subscription, sync your transactions, and provide budgeting features.
  • To Improve RosaFinance: We look at how features are used (like how many people use voice input or receipt scanning) to make the app better. We never use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Services We Use

  • Google Firebase: Handles sign-in, stores your data securely, and helps us find and fix crashes.
  • RevenueCat: Manages your subscription and in-app purchases.
  • OpenAI: Reads your receipt photos through our secure servers to extract transaction details. We don't keep the original photos.
  • WishKit: Powers the in-app feature request and voting system.

Where We Store Your Data

  • Location: All your data is stored in the Netherlands (European Union) on secure Google Cloud servers.
  • Receipt Photos: Sent securely to our EU servers for processing, then immediately deleted after we extract the transaction details.
  • Your Account: We keep your financial records as long as your account is active. You can delete them anytime, or contact us to delete your entire account.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can access, export, correct, or delete it anytime. You can also turn off location permissions in iOS Settings whenever you want. To request account deletion or a data export, email us at info@rosemarysolutions.com.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data during transmission and storage. Your information is secured with the same technology that banks and major services use.

Children

RosaFinance is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
