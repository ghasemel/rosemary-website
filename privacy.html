Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-01

MoneyLeaf is a product by Rosemary Solutions. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, where we process it, how long we keep it, and your choices. If anything here conflicts with an app‑store specific disclosure, the stricter disclosure applies.

Data We Collect

  • Contact Info: Email address (from Sign in with Apple/Google).
  • Identifiers: Firebase UID, RevenueCat App User ID, device identifier (IDFV).
  • Purchases: Subscription and purchase history (via RevenueCat).
  • Financial Data: Expenses, income, assets, debts, budgets you create in the app.
  • Location: Precise location at the time you add a transaction (to tag entries and show on a map/country view).
  • User Content: Text notes you add and feature requests/votes (WishKit).
  • Photos/Videos: Receipt images you capture for extraction.
  • Audio: Voice recordings captured to create transactions via transcription.
  • Diagnostics: Crash and performance data to improve reliability.

How We Use Data

  • App functionality: Authenticate, manage subscriptions, create and sync transactions, tag entries with location, and provide budgeting features.
  • Analytics: Aggregate usage metrics (e.g., features used, count of processed photos/audio) to improve MoneyLeaf. We do not use your data for third‑party advertising or cross‑app tracking.

Third Parties We Use

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database/Dataconnect, and diagnostics.
  • RevenueCat: In‑app purchases and subscription management.
  • OpenAI: Processes receipt photos and audio via our backend to extract structured data. Originals are not stored by us.
  • WishKit: In‑app feature requests and voting.

Data Storage & Retention

  • Region: Hosted in the EU (Google Cloud/Firebase, region europe‑west4).
  • Receipts & Audio: Sent securely to our EU backend for real‑time processing and discarded once processed.
  • Account Data: Financial records and identifiers are retained while your account is active and until you delete them or request deletion.

Your Rights

You can access, export, correct, or delete your data. You can also withdraw location permissions at any time in iOS Settings. To request account deletion or data export, contact us at info@rosemarysolutions.com.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We apply least‑privilege access and encryption‑at‑rest where supported by our providers.

Children

MoneyLeaf is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
