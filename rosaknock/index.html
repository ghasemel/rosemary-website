Set up on iPhone, run on Watch

Create and customize your activities on your iPhone, then run them directly on your Apple Watch — no phone needed during your session.

  • Create activities — Choose periodic knocks or interval-based timing
  • Customize settings — Set duration, knock intervals, and more
  • Run on Watch — Start activities directly from your wrist
  • Feel the tap — Gentle haptic knocks keep you on track

Two ways to stay aware

Choose the timing style that works best for your activity.

  • Periodic knocks — Get a gentle tap at regular intervals (e.g., every 2 minutes)
  • Interval timing — Perfect for structured sessions with work and rest periods

Simple on your wrist
