Simple by design

Open the app, pick your total duration and knock interval, tap Start. That's it.

  • Pick your duration — 1, 5, 10, 15, or 20 minutes
  • Choose your interval — Get knocked every 10 seconds to 2 minutes
  • Feel the tap — Rosa knocks at each interval with a gentle haptic
  • Done — Final knock when time's up

Meet Rosa

Rosa isn't just a timer — she's a friendly companion. At each interval, she performs a short "knock-knock" animation on your watch face, making time awareness feel personal, not clinical.
