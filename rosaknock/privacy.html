Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Rosa Knock is a product by Rosemary Solutions. This is a simple privacy policy for a simple app — because Rosa Knock doesn't collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

What We Collect

Nothing.

Rosa Knock runs entirely on your Apple Watch. We don't collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

What Stays on Your Device

  • Timer settings: Your chosen duration and interval preferences are stored locally on your watch.
  • Session data: When you start a session, the app runs on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere.

If you delete the app, all local settings are removed. There's nothing on our servers to delete because there are no servers.

No Accounts

Rosa Knock doesn't require sign-in, accounts, or any registration. You download it, use it, and that's it.

No Backend

Rosa Knock is a fully standalone Apple Watch app. It doesn't connect to the internet, doesn't sync to any cloud service, and doesn't communicate with any external servers.

No Analytics

We don't track how you use the app. We don't know how many sessions you run, what durations you pick, or how often you use it. Your usage is private.

Children

Rosa Knock doesn't collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, reach out to us at info@rosemarysolutions.com.
